We evaluated long-term habitat quality dynamics and their spatial heterogeneity to inform ecological restoration and conservation planning.
The analysis includes:
- Quantification of habitat quality changes from 1992 to 2022 using the InVEST model driven by land use and land cover data  
- Spatiotemporal analysis of habitat quality evolution across Guangxi Province  
- Spatial autocorrelation analysis to characterize clustering patterns and spatial dependence  
- Interpretation of ecological implications for biodiversity conservation and restoration prioritization
